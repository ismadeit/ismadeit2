Our Courses

At ItsMadeIt, we are committed to helping youth develop skills that matter. Our courses are designed to foster leadership, ethics, and community engagement. Whether you're looking to improve your academic performance, enhance your ethical decision-making, or get involved in your community, we have a course for you.

Featured Courses

Leadership Skills Development

This course helps you develop key leadership skills such as communication, decision-making, and team management. Learn how to lead by example and inspire others.

Ethics and Integrity

Explore the importance of ethics in leadership and how to uphold integrity in all aspects of life. This course will teach you how to make ethical decisions and stand firm in your values.

Time Management for Students

Learn how to manage your time effectively so you can succeed in your academic and personal life. This course provides practical tools for staying organized and focused.

Community Engagement

Get involved with your local community! This course provides guidance on how to create positive change through community service, volunteerism, and building relationships with others.

Building Emotional Intelligence

Starting Your Own Business

This course is perfect for young entrepreneurs. Learn the basics of starting a business, from planning and marketing to financial management.
